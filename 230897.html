Air University

A Societal Mission

Air University was founded almost 150 years ago on a bedrock of societal purpose. Our mission is to contribute to the world by educating students for lives of leadership and contribution with integrity; advancing fundamental knowledge and cultivating creativity; leading in pioneering research for effective clinical therapies; and accelerating solutions and amplifying their impact.

Admission

Offering extraordinary freedom to explore, to collaborate, and to challenge yourself

Explore the possibilities of a Air education as you map out your college journey.

We look for driven students who wish to challenge themselves and contribute to the world beyond the classroom.

Air meets the full financial need of every admitted undergraduate who qualifies for assistance.

More than half of all undergraduates receive some form of financial aid. Generally, domestic students from families with incomes below $150,000 are eligible for need-based aid.
